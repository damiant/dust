dust works offline, meaning that it will work at the burn. It also means that we don't send or receive data and we dont track anything you do (privacy policy).

If you are wondering why we can't release location information earlier, it is because Burning Man requires it as part of their terms of service.

Don't worry though, all the camp and art locations will appear even if you dont have cell service.

Data for dust comes from Burning Man via their Innovation program.

For regional burns we provide a platform to manage events, camps and art. This is free for regionals.

We also get data from Rock Star Librarian, thanks Kate!
